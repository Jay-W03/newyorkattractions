New York, New York! Tourist Attractions: Top 3

The Big Apple

The big apple, the city of wonders. Wondering where to go or what to do in this huge city? Well you have come to the right place. From luxurious restaurants to extravagant stores, New York City is the place to be. No matter what the season, there are an endless amount of things to do and see in New York. Here are a few places that will make your New York experience exceptional.

1) Statue of Liberty:

Let freedom ring! The Statue of Liberty was a gift from France to America during the American Revolution. This statue continues to be a symbol of liberty, freedom and hope today. While touring the Statue of Liberty, you have the option to stop by Ellis Island and look around the Immigration Museum. Many people wait for a ferry to carry them from Battery Park to Liberty Island where the statue is located. You can see NY’s skyline from the statue’s crown. Make sure to reserve your visit at least 3 weeks in advance.

2) Times Square:

Filled with bright lights and much entertainment, Times Square is a major tourist attraction. There are many fun places to go in Times Square such as Madame Tussauds wax museum and Ripley’s Believe it or Not. The annual New Year’s Eve ball drop has been a staple event in the area since 1907. You'll get a chance to see Times Square light up at night with its many billboards and glowing signs. Times Square is a place you most definitely do not want to miss.

3) Central Park:

This big park located in the center of the city gives you a little break from the city life and allows you to be surrounded by nature. The park has many activities at no cost which makes it such as great place to go with family and friends. Some of the places in the park include the Central Park Zoo, Strawberry Fields and Belvedere Castle.
